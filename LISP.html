LISP

This is the LISP page.

Creation

Created in 1958 by John McCarthy.

Evolution

One of the oldest programming languages, known for its unique syntax and powerful features like homoiconicity and metaprogramming.

Usage

Historically used in artificial intelligence research, symbolic computation, and language development.

Current State

Still used in niche areas like AI research, but has declined in mainstream usage compared to newer languages.
